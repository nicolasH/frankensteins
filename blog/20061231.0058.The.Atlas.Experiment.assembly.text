The Atlas experiment is one of the <a href="http://public.web.cern.ch/Public/Content/Chapters/AboutCERN/CERNFuture/LHCExperiments/LHCExperiments-en.html%22">five experiments</a> built into the LHC. The biggest hope of the LHC is to detect Higg's Boson. Each experiment have a brother which should be able to corroborate any results using a different method.
